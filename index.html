General Guidance for All Athletes

Consistent Training

Regular, structured practice is key to athletic improvement. Focus on progressive overload.

Adequate Recovery

7-9 hours of quality sleep and active recovery days are essential for peak performance.

Mental Preparation

Visualization, focus techniques, and stress management are crucial for competition.

Proper Hydration

Maintain optimal hydration before, during, and after training sessions.

Essential Diet for Athletes

Carbohydrates

Primary fuel source (55-65% of calories). Whole grains, fruits, vegetables.

Proteins

Muscle repair (15-25% of calories). Lean meats, fish, eggs, legumes.

Healthy Fats

Energy storage (20-35% of calories). Avocados, nuts, olive oil.

Hydration

Water + electrolytes. 2-3 liters daily, more during intense training.

Vitamins & Minerals

Iron, calcium, Vitamin D. Crucial for energy production and bone health.

Timing

Pre-workout: 2-3 hours before. Post-workout: Within 30-45 minutes.
