Sport-Specific Diets


Endurance Sports (Running, Swimming, Cycling)

Daily Requirements:

  • Carbs: 6-10g per kg body weight
  • Protein: 1.2-1.6g per kg body weight
  • Fat: 20-35% of total calories
  • Hydration: 500ml per hour of training

Sample Meal Plan:

  • Breakfast: Oatmeal with berries, nuts, and Greek yogurt
  • Pre-workout: Banana and energy bar
  • Post-workout: Protein shake and rice cakes
  • Lunch: Grilled chicken with quinoa and vegetables
  • Dinner: Salmon with sweet potato and steamed greens

Strength Sports (Weightlifting, Football)

Daily Requirements:

  • Protein: 1.6-2.2g per kg body weight
  • Carbs: 4-7g per kg body weight
  • Fat: 20-35% of total calories
  • Creatine: 5g daily supplementation

Sample Meal Plan:

  • Breakfast: 4-egg omelet with spinach and whole wheat toast
  • Pre-workout: Greek yogurt with honey
  • Post-workout: Whey protein and fast-digesting carbs
  • Lunch: Lean beef with brown rice and broccoli
  • Dinner: Turkey breast with quinoa and mixed vegetables

Skill Sports (Basketball, Tennis)

Daily Requirements:

  • Carbs: 5-8g per kg body weight
  • Protein: 1.4-1.8g per kg body weight
  • Fat: 25-35% of total calories
  • Hydration: Focus on electrolytes

Sample Meal Plan:

  • Breakfast: Protein pancakes with fruit
  • Pre-game: Energy gel and sports drink
  • Post-game: Chocolate milk and protein bar
  • Lunch: Tuna salad wrap with avocado
  • Dinner: Chicken stir-fry with noodles
